Gordo

Here is a short story about Gordo who wants to be a chef. Gordo is from a small town in Louisiana and found a love for food at a young age. Ever since he moved out he has missed his home food and wants to learn how to make it himself.

Gordo's Love for food

Gordo started in a small town called Choudrant in Northern Louisana. He always loved his cajun food and grew up with it. Eventually Gordo left and went to college and some how ended up at the University of Arkansas at Little Rock. Crazy, I know! Gord did not want to continue to eat the cafeteria food for the rest of his education at the college so he bagan to learn how to cook the cajun food he always loved and missed from home.

What dish should Gordo make?

Dirty Rice
Dirty rice is a traditional Louisiana Creole dish made from white rice which gets a "dirty" color from being cooked with small pieces of pork, beef or chicken, green bell pepper, celery, and onion, and spiced with cayenne and black pepper.
Jambalaya
Jambalaya is a popular dish consisting mainly of meat and vegetables mixed with rice.
Gumbo

Gordo Needs Ingredients

Gordo begins his journey for cajun food by going to the most obscure grocery store, Walmart. Gordo needs quite the sum of ingredients for his first dish, dirty rice, so he makes a list of ingredients.
  • Lean ground beef
  • Diced onions
  • Diced Bell Pepper
  • 1 Cup of diced Celery
  • 3 Cloves of Minced Garlic
  • Cajun Seasoning
  • White Rice
  • Beef Broth
  • Chopped Green Onion

Time to Cook

Gordo begins to execute the instructions that he remembered his mother doing.
  1. Add ground beef to a dutch oven or heavy bottomed pan set over medium heat. Crumble with a spatula and cook 4-5 minutes until no pink remains.
  2. Add onion, garlic, green pepper, celery, Cajun seasoning, and salt. Stir until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add rice and beef broth. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer covered about 20 to 23 minutes or until rice is cooked.
  4. Allow to sit for 5 to 10 minutes, then fluff with a fork. Garnish with green onions before serving.

The End Result

Gordo has finally completed his dish after the proper steps and ingredients were used. He loves it! Gordo is finally made food that reminds him of his home and is more than happy. Gordo things he will use this recipe all the time now and try more dishes soon.
